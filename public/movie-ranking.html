The Best Movies According to Jobelyn

My Top 3 Movies of All Time

Stranger Things

This movie is an American Science Fiction Horror Drama Television series created by the Duffer Brothers. It is about a group of young friends that witness supernatural forces and secret government exploits. As they were curious, they search for answers and unravel a series of mysteries in their town. I really love this movie and I'm looking forward to its next season.

The Space Between Us

This movie is an American Romantic Science Fiction Film directed by Peter Chelsom and written by Allan Loeb. Gardner Elliot, the first human born on Mars, begins an online friendship with Tulsa, a teen in Colorado. On his maiden voyage to Earth, he finally gets to experience all the joys and wonders of the world he could only read about. But they discover that Gardner's organs can't withstand the atmosphere on Earth. The interplanetary visitor races against time to unravel the mysteries of how he came to be, and where he belongs in the universe. This is a good movie, I would personally recommend it to teenagers.

Matilda The Musical

This movie is a musical fantasy comedy drama film directed by Matthew Warchus. It's about a young girl named Matilda Wormwood, who has an incredible intellect and magic powers, including the ability to levitate objects. She is a lover of books, and can read stories meant for adults by the age of four. Her clear intelligence goes unnoticed by her uncaring family, who often treat her badly. I'd recommend mostly this for kids, it is fun to watch.
